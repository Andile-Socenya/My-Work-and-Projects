Jabulani Magubane

CEO

Jabulani Magubane is the co-founder and director of MGT and has more than 21 years diverse business management experience and skils. he is 49 years old, holds Bcom and LLB degrees from the University of South Africa and a number of short course certificates from various institutions.

Born eZola 3 in Soweto attended school Ezakheni towmship in ladysmith KZN and matriculated in 1993 at CML Centre in Orlando east. in 1995 joined the South African National Defence force and between 1998 and 2000 he served his three years of articles as an accountant and auditor at Eksteen Louw & Badenhorst Inc, a firm of Charted Accountants (SAICA) certified him as an Associate Accounting Technician AAT(SA).

He then started an accounting, tax and business consulting firm Magubane Advisory Incorporated which is still in business to date. in 2004 he did a national home builders registration council (NHBRC) course and established Magubane Construction Projects(Pty) Ltd, a company that has developed and built quality houses some of which were approved and financed by major financial institutions.

During the same year he did a short course through which he qualified as a Certified Estate Agent(CEA ) registered under the thenEstate agency Affairs board and established an estate agency firm Magubane Properties (Pty) Ltd were sold

in 2008 he completed debt counsellor's training course through which he carried on debt counselling business in Johannesburg and Durban.

security
in 2010 he scaled down his business operations and joined Maesela Incorporated Attorneys as a candidate attorney. He is scheduled to write attorney's admission board exam in march 2023.

in 2015 he did private security training and registered with the Private Security Industry Regulation Authrity(PSIRA) and established Magubane Security (Pty) Ltd. In 2018 he co-founded Magubane Technologies (Pty) Ltd together with Nqaba Khumalo which now operated as a division of MGT
